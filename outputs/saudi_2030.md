# Vision 2030 – Saudi Arabia

## Comprehensive Overview

Saudi Vision 2030 is a strategic framework aimed at reducing Saudi Arabia’s dependence on oil, diversifying its economy, and developing public service sectors such as health, education, infrastructure, recreation, and tourism.

---

## Three Main Pillars

### 1. A Vibrant Society

- **Increase Umrah visitors** from 8 million to 30 million annually, establishing Saudi Arabia as a premier religious and cultural destination.  
- **Establish the world’s largest Islamic museum** to preserve and showcase Islamic heritage and history.  
- **Double the number of Saudi UNESCO World Heritage Sites** to promote cultural preservation and tourism.  
- **Promote healthier lifestyles** by increasing citizens exercising at least once weekly from 13% to 40%.  
- **Develop three Saudi cities** to rank among the world’s top 100 cities for quality of life and livability.  
- **Expand cultural and entertainment opportunities**, including cinemas, concerts, and sports events.  
- **Strengthen family values and social cohesion** while preserving Islamic identity and moderate religious teachings.

---

### 2. A Thriving Economy

- **Reduce economic dependence on oil revenues** by diversifying into non-oil sectors worth trillions in new economic value.  
- **Increase SME (Small and Medium Enterprise) contribution to GDP** from 20% to 35% by fostering entrepreneurship.  
- **Reduce unemployment rate** from 11.6% to 7% through private sector job creation and economic opportunities.  
- **Increase Foreign Direct Investment (FDI)** from 3.8% to 5.7% of GDP by creating a business-friendly regulatory environment.  
- **Partially privatize Saudi Aramco** through a 5% Initial Public Offering to establish a sovereign wealth fund supporting transformation.  
- **Develop new economic engines** in tourism, entertainment, logistics, technology, mining, and renewable energy.  
- **Position at least five Saudi universities** among the world’s top 200 institutions for research and innovation.  
- **Increase women’s workforce participation** from 17% to 37%, unlocking significant economic potential and talent.

---

### 3. An Ambitious Nation

- **Improve government efficiency, transparency, and accountability** through comprehensive institutional reforms and digital transformation.  
- **Streamline public services** with over 80% e-government service delivery, reducing bureaucracy and improving citizen experience.  
- **Implement anti-corruption initiatives**, recovering billions in misused funds and establishing ethical governance standards.  
- **Enhance government performance measurement systems** to ensure effective delivery of public services and programs.  
- **Foster civic engagement and volunteering culture**, encouraging citizens to contribute to national development goals.  
- **Strengthen public administration capabilities** and modernize government operations for 21st-century challenges.

---

## Key Mega-Projects

- **NEOM:**  
  A $500 billion futuristic smart city in northwestern Saudi Arabia featuring sustainable living and advanced technology.

- **Red Sea Project:**  
  A luxury tourism destination encompassing 90 pristine islands, promoting sustainable eco-tourism.

- **Diriyah Gate:**  
  A cultural and heritage development in Riyadh celebrating Saudi history and architectural traditions.

- **Qiddiya:**  
  An entertainment, sports, and cultural mega-project creating the Middle East’s premier entertainment destination.

---

## Vision Realization Programs (VRPs)

Thirteen specialized programs implement Vision 2030 objectives, including:

- National Transformation Program  
- Financial Sector Development Program  
- Public Investment Fund Program  
- Privatization Program  
- Strategic Partnership Program  
- National Industrial Development and Logistics Program  
- Housing Program  
- Quality of Life Program  
- Human Capability Development Program  
- Health Sector Transformation Program  
- Pilgrim Experience Program  
- National Character Enrichment Program  
- Public Investment Fund’s Portfolio of Domestic Investments Program  

---

## Strategic Advantages

Saudi Arabia leverages three core competitive advantages:

1. **Cultural and Religious Significance:** Serving as the heart of the Arab and Islamic worlds.  
2. **Investment Capabilities:** Possessing leading investment capacity through sovereign wealth and natural resources.  
3. **Strategic Geographic Position:** Connecting Asia, Europe, and Africa for trade and commerce.
